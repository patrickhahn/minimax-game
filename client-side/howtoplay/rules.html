Play Leaderboard How to Play Login
  • You begin by choosing which computer science professor to play against.
    • Higher level professors make smarter tile selections, but each turn will take more time
  • The goal of the game is to earn as many points as possible
  • You earn points for each tile you own
    • The point value of each tile (1-99) is printed on the tile
  • Tiles owned by you are dark blue, those owned by the CPU are pink
  • To own a tile, you click on it, which will change the tile to your color
  • If you click an unclaimed tile that is touching another tile you own vertically or horizontally (not diagonally) it converts all enemy-owned surrounding tiles to your color
  • The game ends when all tiles on the board are claimed
